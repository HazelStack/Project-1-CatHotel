Medications and Dietary Supplements

Medications and dietary supplements are administered as requested. Additional charges apply for administering food supplements, vitamins, medications, insulin, other injections, and subcutaneous hydration therapy (fluids).

Guest Health & Vaccination Requirements

The health of our guests is of paramount importance. We require a veterinarian's verification that your cat is up to date with respiratory (FVRCP) and Rabies vaccinations. The FVRCP initial series must be complete prior to boarding. Vaccine records can be emailed to info@purrfectstay.com or uploaded directly to our client portal. Kittens are welcome at four months of age or older, and by eight months must be neutered/spayed. We cannot accommodate cats that are FIV or FELV positive.
